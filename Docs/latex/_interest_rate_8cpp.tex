Computes the area under a line segment of the interest rate curve. 

Handles both positive and negative rates and computes the area under the curve for a segment defined by two points ((t\+\_\+1, r\+\_\+1)) and ((t\+\_\+2, r\+\_\+2)). If the rates have opposite signs, splits the area at the zero crossing.
